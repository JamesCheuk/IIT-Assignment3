Skills and Jobs

To deliver Wake Up, four specific roles will be required to deliver a working application to the market.

Project Manager

Job Purpose

A Project Manager is required to plan and manage the delivery of the Wake App project.

A Project Manager is responsible for product planning and execution. They will need to gather and prioritise product and customer requirements. They will also need to work closely with other departments (development, testing etc) to ensure the product and customer requirements are met.

The project manager needs to ensure that priority is given to the most critical outcomes for the project and communicate any decisions made in a way all parties can accept.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage the product from concept to design, testing and support.
  • Delivery of an operating plan
  • Communicate the plan to all relevant participants
  • Continuously gather and analyse relevant data to the product to refine the product plan
  • Assisting in solving day to day problems
  • Ensure the project meets short-term and long-term objectives

Skill Requirements

  • Exceptional communication skills, as they will be in contact with a wide range of people
  • Good time management, ensure the project meets deadlines
  • Basic understanding of code
  • Understand the project's user lifecycle

UX Designer

Job Purpose

The role of User Experience (UX) Designer is required on the project for the Wake App.

This role will be responsible for conducting customer research to develop the App’s overall interactive and visual design.

A human centre design approach will be undertaken to gather analysis from prospective customers to ensure that it responds to their needs and is appealing to use. The UX Designer will be required to research the following five stages to ensure that the best possible solution is developed:

  1. Empathise

    Engages with focus groups and prospective customers to understand to gather their insights to identify their requirements and how Wake App can be used in their day-to-day life.

  2. Define

    Develops the personas associated with the prospective customers that will use Wake App and defines the needs that they require.

  3. Ideate

    Leading the engagement with the other team members develops solutions options associated with the design and development of Wake App.

  4. Prototype

    Designs the flows and wireframes for Wake-App that will be used to develop the end-to-end solution for the project.

  5. Test

    Validates the process flows and wireframes with perspectives customers and iterates design based on the feedback provided.

Key Responsibilities

The responsibilities of the role include:

  • Facilitate inception workshops and ideation sessions with stakeholders and focus groups
  • Employ human-centered design principles to design solutions that appeal to user needs through research gathered
  • Develop personas associated that identify users’ needs and objectives
  • Convert user research and usability findings into design outputs
  • Develop sketches, flowcharts, wireframes, and prototypes to be tested, validated, and promote robust discussions with the team
  • Support the ongoing development of the solution through ongoing collaboration with the project team (developer(s) and tester(s) through consistent iteration

Skill Requirements

The skills required for the role include:

  • Knowledge of HTML/CSS
  • Proficient in Design Software to create wireframes (AdodeX, UXPin, PhotoShop, Invision)
  • Understanding of the Design Thinking processes (Empathise, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test)
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to lead collaboration with other team members
  • Agile Delivery experience and an understanding of incremental delivery

Lead Developer

Job Purpose

The Lead Developer is needed to build the application’s back end, so there is a product to release to the public.

The lead developer will be responsible for developing the software for the project, update the team on any difficulties encountered during development and ideas and methods they have tried to solve said difficulties.

They will also be working alongside the tester to ensure that the software is thoroughly tested before it is released, so time can be better spent working on future features to add to the application.

They will also be the one who serves as a connection between the development team to other business-oriented departments. They will assist in planning the product development timelines.

Key Responsibilities

  • Clarify any uncertainties regarding the application before starting development with related parties
  • Provide enough design to ensure the essential feature of the application is usable while still being the highest possible quality
  • Thoroughly test the software to reduce the number of bugs
  • Attend meetings to update other members on how the development is going
  • Support and maintain the software for future feature additions or receipt of a bug report

Skill Requirements

  • Understands high-level architectural designs and patterns
  • Experience building full solutions
  • Experience with Java or similar languages
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Understanding of other disciplines such as product development, management, etc

Tester

Job Purpose

A Tester is required to support the delivery of the Wake App project.

The tester will be responsible for coordinating the planning, management, and execution of testing throughout the different stages of the project's delivery lifecycle. The purpose of the role is to ensure that the quality process is put in place and actioned to ensure that Wake App is delivered without any significant defects that may impact its performance.

There will be two phases of testing for the Wake App project that the tester must actively support; System Testing and User Acceptance Testing.

  • System testing is the process of verifying that all the hardware and software components have been successfully brought together and meet the specified requirements.

  • User acceptance testing verifies that the solution meets the users' needs and performances as required within the operating environment.

Key Responsibilities

The responsibilities of the role include:

  • Develop the overall test plan and approach that is to be undertaken throughout the project life cycle.
  • Define the test scenarios for testing in consultation with the project team and users
  • Identify and document the test cases that are required to be actioned to verify that Wake App meets the user acceptance criteria captured in the business requirement and solution specification
  • Maintains the defect log through the project lifecycle. Ensures that each defect is monitored through the testing lifecycle to a successful resolution
  • Coordinates the execution of testing with the relevant stakeholders. Executes the test cases where appropriate.
  • Prepares the Final Test Summary report and facilitates the process of signoff with all relevant stakeholders.

Skill Requirements

The skills required for the role include:

  • Good communication skills to engage and collaborate with all appropriate stakeholders
  • Strong time management skills to ensure that critical activities for testing are aligned to the overall delivery timeline
  • Understanding of QA Tools and how they support the testing lifecycle within a project
  • Attention to detail and quality
  • Solid problem-solving skills
  • Solid problem-solving skills
