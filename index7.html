Testing

To test Wake App, we recommend that three phases of testing are undertaken to ensure that a workable solution can be delivered. The phases would consist of:

Usability Testing

Utilising a focus, we’d provide them with our wireframes to give feedback on the following aspects of our project:

  • The user experience of our design
  • Identify opportunities to improve the functionality of the App: and,
  • Test the project idea to ensure that it meets the objectives of our project.

Based on the group's feedback, we’d adapt our designs and requirements to ensure Wake App is easy to use and responds to the user’s needs.

System Testing

Post completion of the App's development and integrating all the components, system testing will be undertaken.

The purpose of the is to ensure that Wake App meets all the purposed design's technical specifications.

User Acceptance Testing

User acceptance testing will be undertaken ideally after completing system testing and before the launch of Wake App to the general public.

User acceptance testing aims to ensure that the App works as per the project's original objectives. The App will be tested with a small of users to ensure that it works as designed in practice. Any identified defects would be addressed or placed in the product backlog to fix in a future release.
